Powerful Features

Everything you need to perfect your Quran memorization

🏠 Your Dashboard

Everything at a glance — hifz progress, streaks, review recommendations, and quick access to continue where you left off.

📖 Check Your Hifz

Pick any page and start reciting from memory. HifzGuide listens, tracks your position, and tells you how well you did — page by page, word by word.

🎙️ Real-Time Follow-Along

Just recite — HifzGuide follows along word by word and highlights your position in the mushaf in real time.

✅ Word-Level Feedback

Get instant feedback on every word. Wrong words and tashkeel errors are detected and highlighted so you know exactly what to fix.

🔄 Self-Correction Detection

Made a mistake and corrected yourself? HifzGuide notices when you re-read and updates your score automatically.

📊 Track Your Progress

See quality ratings for every surah and page — Perfect, Good, or needs review. Smart scheduling tells you what to revisit and when.

🔍 Voice & Text Search

Search the Quran by typing Arabic text — or by reciting a verse aloud. Find any passage instantly.

🔒

Fully On-Device AI

All speech recognition runs locally on your iPhone using the Apple Neural Engine. Your recitation never leaves your device.
